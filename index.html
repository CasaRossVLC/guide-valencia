THE PAELLA QUEENS

Paella is Valencia's most iconic dish, with many variations (Senyoret, lobster, mixed seafood), but the most representative is the Valenciana, with chicken, rabbit, and sometimes snails.

Traditionally eaten for lunch, you can also find a few places to enjoy it for dinner.

Important note: generally, you need to book in advance to have paella, especially if you order the classic one, which must be requested when booking.
